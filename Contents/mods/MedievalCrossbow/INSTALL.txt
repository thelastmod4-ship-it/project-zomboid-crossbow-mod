This mod is designed for Project Zomboid Build 42.19 and later.

Install Instructions:
1. Subscribe to the mod on Steam Workshop OR
2. Place the MedievalCrossbow folder in your Zomboid/mods/ directory
3. Enable the mod when creating a new world

The mod adds:
- Medieval Crossbow (2-handed ranged weapon)
- Medieval Arrows (reusable ammunition)
- Crafting recipes for both items
